## What is Prompt Engineering, Anyway? ü§î

At its core, Prompt Engineering is the art and science of crafting effective inputs (prompts) to guide AI models, especially Large Language Models (LLMs), toward generating desired and optimal outputs. It's the secret sauce that transforms a raw AI into a powerful, precise, and creative problem-solver.
